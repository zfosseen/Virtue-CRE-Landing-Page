FAQ

Commonly asked questions.

Who owns the software from a build?
We act as an integrated tech partner, which means anything we build on your system belongs to you. Your data always remains exclusively yours.
I've never used AI before. Where should I start?
No worries. Start with a single workflow, one task that could be automated to save you time or capital. Once you are comfortable with that, we help you move into more integrated systems.
How do I get started?
Think about a process in your business you want to bring AI into, then reach out for an introduction meeting. We will walk you through the rest from there.
How long until something is live?
Most first builds are up and running in two to six weeks, on your real deals, inside the tools you already use.
What does it cost?
Most engagements run $20,000 to $40,000 depending on scope, and your first build is backed by a 30-day money-back guarantee.
What tools do you work with?
We build into the systems you already use, from Outlook, Excel, and SharePoint to platforms like Yardi, AppFolio, CoStar, and Juniper Square.
